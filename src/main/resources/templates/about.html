About Smart Contact Manager

User Dashboard

This is the main dashboard where users can manage their contacts, view important information, and perform various tasks.

Contact List

The contact list displays all the contacts stored in the user's account, providing quick access to contact details and options for editing or deleting contacts.

User Information

This section contains the user's personal information, such as name, email, and profile picture. Users can update their profile information and manage account settings.

Contact Details

When viewing individual contacts, users can see detailed information about each contact, including name, phone number, email address, and any additional notes.
